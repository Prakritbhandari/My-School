Aryaman Vikram Birla Institute of Learning was established on 30th March 2004. It is an English medium co-educational day school located in the pollution-free environs of Haldwani.

The school functions from 0730 hours to 0200 hours and comprises classes Lower KG to XII. It is run by Prakash Education Society, a pioneer in the field of education.

Our institution believes in the inculcation of right values with a firm focus on holistic development.

The school provides a wide and varied campus life to its students that combine the following: Potent mix of co-curricular and sporting activities complementing the formal learning process Diverse programme schedule to help students give expression to their interests Emphasis on developing all-round personality Core values:

- Academic excellence

- Development of skills

- Character formation

- Affiliation & Certification

The school is recognized by the State Government. It is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi.
